About us

We are committed to advancing the objectives of eye transplantation across India. We were founded in 2022.

Our goal is to spread awareness across India using public service announcements.

We are prepared to address all inquiries regarding eye donation by phone or email.

In order to encourage more people to give their eyes, we are also prepared to personally respond to inquiries.
