Mission for Vision

Mission for Vision(MFV) is among the larger NGOs working to eradicate avoidable blindness across the country. It does so by enabling high-quality, comprehensive eye care to the most disadvantaged communities; human resource development; research; and policy engagement in India, Nigeria, and Bhutan.Blindness is largely a consequence of poverty and people with visual impairment are more likely to become economically challenged if eye care is inaccessible.The certainty that eradicating avoidable blindness enhances productivity and leads to social and economic equality powered MFV to provide the underprivileged communities with access to the right treatment at the right time.
